# Tomato soup recipe


## Ingredients

- **2 tablespoons** olive oil or unsalted butter
- **1** medium onion, diced
- **2** garlic cloves, minced
- **2 cans** (28 oz / 800g each) whole peeled tomatoes or diced tomatoes
- **2 cups** (about 475ml) vegetable or chicken broth
- **1 cup** (240ml) heavy cream or coconut milk for a vegan option
- **1 teaspoon** sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
- **Salt and black pepper** to taste
- **Fresh basil** chopped, for garnish
- **Crusty bread** or grilled cheese for serving


## Instructions

1. **Sauté the Vegetables:**
   - In a large pot, heat the olive oil or butter over medium heat. Add the
     diced onion and garlic. Cook for about 5 minutes until the onion is
     translucent and fragrant.

2. **Add the Tomatoes:**
   - Add the tomatoes with their juices to the pot. Use a wooden spoon to break
     up any whole tomatoes. Stir in the broth and season with salt and pepper.
     Add the sugar if using. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

3. **Simmer:**
   - Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let the soup simmer for about 20
     minutes, stirring occasionally.

4. **Blend the Soup:**
   - Use an immersion blender to puree the soup directly in the pot until
     smooth. Alternatively, blend the soup in batches using a regular blender.
     Be careful when blending hot liquids and allow steam to escape.

5. **Add Cream:**
   - Stir in the heavy cream or coconut milk and season with additional salt
     and pepper to taste. Heat the soup until it is just warmed through; do not
     boil after adding the cream.

6. **Serve:**
   - Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh basil. Serve with crusty
     bread or grilled cheese on the side.
