# Caprese Salad

## Ingredients

- Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
- Ripe tomatoes, sliced
- Fresh basil leaves
- Extra virgin olive oil
- Balsamic vinegar (optional)
- Salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

- Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella into similar thickness rounds (about 1/4 inch).
- Arrange alternating slices of tomato and mozzarella on a serving platter.
- Tuck fresh basil leaves between the tomato and mozzarella slices.
- Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil (and balsamic vinegar if desired).
- Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.
- Serve immediately at room temperature.
